Project Overview

Overview

Application Purpose: Website to promote advertisement for a photography business/company.

Intended Users: People who want to hire a photographer for events.


Content Overview:

  • Home page
    • To introduce the site and catch the user’s attention.
    • The hero image.
    • Small bio.
  • About page
    • Bio of the photographer.
  • Portfolio page
    • To showcase the photographer’s work.
    • Gallery of photos.
    • Lightbox gallery modal.
  • Services page
    • To inform users about the services provided by the photographer.
    • List of services, pricing details.
  • Contact page
    • To provide a means of communication for potential clients to get in touch.
    • Name, email, phone number.
