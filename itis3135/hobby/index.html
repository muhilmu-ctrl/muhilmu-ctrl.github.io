Welcome to my hobby site!

Reading is one of my favorite hobbies. I enjoy losing myself in stories, whether it's novels, comics, or manga. Each one has its own things: novels are great for deep and thorough stories, comics are colorful and entertaining, and manga have amazing action and art.

I love reading because it helps me relax, use my imagination, and learn new things.


Some of my favorite genres include:

When I Read

I usually read whenever I want to take a break from working on assignments or if I get bored.

During breaks or holidays, I usually set aside a solid hour or so to read uninterrupted.

On busy days, I might only have a few minutes to read while in-between classes or when waiting in line to get food, so I keep digital copies of some books on my phone so I can read wherever I am.

Where I Read

I read pretty much anywhere I have a moment to myself. At home, it's usually at my desk or on the sofa. On campus, it's usually when I'm eating lunch, when I'm waiting on my next class, or waiting for the bus.

I get my books from a few different places. I go to libraries to find new books I may be interested in, bookstores to find physical copies of books I want to keep, and online if I can't find what I'm looking for.


Where I get my books:

  • Local Bookstores
  • Libraries
  • Online Websites

How I Read

I read in a few different ways depending on my mood and where I am. If I'm home, I prefer to hold and read physical books. If I'm outside, I'll use my phone to read digital comics or manga.

I also use reading websites to find books, comics, or manga online. It makes it easier to find something new to read, access wherever I am, and read whenever I feel like reading.

Why I Read

I genuinely enjoy reading becasue it gives me a break from the real world. It provides characters, places, and experiences I wouldn't get in everyday life.

No matter if it's a novel full of suspense, a comic full of vibrant art, or a manga full of action, each different format offers unique ways to explore new stories and ideas. Reading feeds/fuels my imagination, helps me unwind and relax, and sometimes teaches me new perspectives I may never have considered otherwise.

One of the main reasons I love to read is how it embraces different and unique ideas and makes them work. The creativity in stories help and inspire me to think differently.
