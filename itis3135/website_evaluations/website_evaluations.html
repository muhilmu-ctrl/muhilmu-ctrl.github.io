Website Evaluations

Samsung

Samsung's website is a global platform for buying mobile devices, home appliances, and electronics.

  • Contrast: Samsung uses strong visual contrast with images of products on light and dark colored backgrounds.
  • Repetition: The design elements, such as fonts, buttons, and how images and information layouts are consistent across the site.
  • Alignment: The site's layout is designed and structured with good grid-alignment which ensures that everything on the pages feel neat and easy on the eyes.
  • Proximity: Navigation bars, product options, and links for support and additional information are grouped in different sections so users can find them and information related to them with ease.

Yale School of Art

The website for the Yale School of Art provides information regarding the degree programs offered by the school.

  • Contrast: The background colors of the headers clash with the page's background colors and other text on the page.
  • Repetition: The pages lack consitency. Some of the pages use different font sizes, styles, and layouts, which breaks the overall flow of the entire website.
  • Alignment: Some of the pages are not aligned, having images and text placed unevenly and/or asymmetrically.
  • Proximity: Some content is spaced too close to unrelated content. Everything on the pages are all on one side.
